This video gives an overview of the training site. It is a screen recording that goes through what you see on the site and what a person needs to do when using the site. It contains descriptive captions and sound. We anticipate that the training site can be used by a person in different ways. Firstly, it can be used by a person at home on their own. Secondly, it can be used in a workshop or group environment where a people try it out with the help of a technical support facilitator in an organisation session, for example hosted by a charitable organisation. Our training site has been developed to work entirely within a browser, you do not need to download anything to get it to run. No personal data is collected through its use.
