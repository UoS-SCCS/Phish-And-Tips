Getting Started

Please provide a name to create your training account, which includes a temporary training email address. This email address is only for use during the following lessons and is separate to your normal email address. It cannot be used to send or receive emails outside of the training site.

Once you have finished the training click the "Finish Training" button at the bottom of the page and your training account and temporary email address will be deleted, you do not need to doing anything further.

Your temporary email address will be of the form name@example.com where name will be the value you provide below. Use your first name, for example, joe, mary, leslie.

Your chosen name cannot have any spaces in it, but don't worry, these will be automatically removed if you do try to enter any of them.
